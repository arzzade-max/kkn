Our Focus Areas

Six pillars that guide our mission toward a better Konkan

Education

Scholarships, digital learning centers, and skill-based education for underprivileged youth across rural Konkan.

Learn more

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups, vocational training, and micro-finance support enabling women to become financially independent.

Learn more

Health & Nutrition

Free medical camps, nutrition awareness, maternal care programs, and mobile health clinics for remote villages.

Learn more

Environment

Tree plantation drives, coastal clean-ups, and promoting eco-friendly practices to protect Konkan's rich biodiversity.

Learn more

Livelihood

Agricultural training, cooperative farming, and market linkages to increase farmers' incomes across the region.

Learn more

Water & Sanitation

Flagship Initiative

The Saksham Program

Saksham — meaning "capable" — is our flagship youth skill development program, designed to bridge the gap between education and employment for rural youth in the Konkan region.

Digital Skills Training

Industry-aligned tech curriculum with hands-on project experience.

Placement Support

Active corporate partnerships for direct job placement on program completion.

Mentorship Network

One-on-one mentoring from industry professionals and community leaders.
